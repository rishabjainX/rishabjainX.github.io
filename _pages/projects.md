# Projects

A selection of things I've built and researched. Most sit at the intersection of
machine learning and public policy.

<div class="project">
  <h3>Adversarial Debiasing for Recidivism Risk Assessment <span class="meta">· Independent research · 2024–present</span></h3>
  <p>Authored a paper using adversarial learning and SHAP explainability to study racial bias in recidivism risk-assessment models. Built and evaluated a classifier that reduced racial disparities across demographic parity, false-positive rate, and false-negative rate metrics.</p>
  <div class="tags"><span>PyTorch</span><span>SHAP</span><span>Adversarial learning</span><span>Algorithmic fairness</span></div>
</div>

<div class="project">
  <h3>Explainable AI for Pneumonia Detection <span class="meta">· ASDRP · 2023–2024</span></h3>
  <p>Worked as an AI/ML researcher at the Aspiring Scholars Directed Research Program, using the Captum library to apply explainable-AI techniques to convolutional neural networks for pneumonia classification. Presented at two public poster symposiums.</p>
  <div class="tags"><span>PyTorch</span><span>Captum</span><span>CNNs</span><span>Medical imaging</span></div>
</div>

<div class="project">
  <h3>Whiteboard — AI Student Dashboard <span class="meta">· Los Altos Hacks '25 · Oct 2024</span></h3>
  <p>Built a dashboard that helps teachers grade handwritten work and deliver personalized feedback. Integrated AWS for scalable storage, wrote a pipeline to digitize handwritten submissions, and used LLM-based feedback generation for individualized education plans and class-wide analysis.</p>
  <div class="tags"><span>LLMs</span><span>AWS</span><span>OCR</span><span>EdTech</span></div>
</div>

<div class="project">
  <h3>Clarify — ML Deblurring for Security Footage <span class="meta">· Congressional App Challenge '24 · Oct 2024</span></h3>
  <p>iOS app that uses machine learning to remove blur from heavily pixelated security footage, aimed at making protection against package theft and robberies accessible from any smartphone. Co-designed the model and the front-end with a team of four.</p>
  <div class="tags"><span>Computer vision</span><span>iOS</span><span>Image super-resolution</span></div>
</div>

<div class="project">
  <h3>Fremont School of Coding <span class="meta">· Co-founder &amp; VP · 2022–2024</span></h3>
  <p>Co-founded a local non-profit providing free computer-science education, prioritizing hands-on experience over traditional teaching. Taught 125+ students over three years and ran an inaugural week-long summer camp in India for 25+ students.</p>
